About Us

Rediscover the Joy of Traditional Toys

Welcome to Desi Toys, where tradition meets play! We bring you beautifully crafted, traditional Indian toys that celebrate India’s rich cultural heritage and support local artisans. Each toy is a unique piece of art, handmade with care and designed to inspire joy and creativity in every generation. Join us in preserving this timeless art and bringing a piece of India's legacy into your home.
History

The Legacy of Indian Toymaking

India's toy-making tradition spans thousands of years, with roots in ancient civilizations and local artistry. Crafted from natural materials like wood, clay, and cloth, these toys represent regional culture, myths, and everyday life. They were designed not only for play but to teach values and tell stories, making them a cherished part of Indian heritage. At Khilona, we celebrate this legacy, bringing traditional toys that connect generations and preserve the artistry of India's past.
Craftsmanship

The Art of Craftsmanship

Every toy in our collection is a piece of art, handmade by skilled artisans who use traditional techniques passed down through generations. From hand-carved woodwork to intricate hand-painted designs, each toy reflects a dedication to quality and heritage. At Desi Toys, we honor the artistry and dedication of India's craftsmen and women, whose hands breathe life into every toy.

Supporting Local Artisans

At Desi Toys, we believe in celebrating the artistry and heritage of traditional Indian toys by supporting the talented local artisans who craft them. These artisans, often inheriting skills passed down through generations, are the heart and soul of our culture. By purchasing from Khilona, you're not only acquiring a beautiful, handmade toy but also helping to sustain these artisans' livelihoods. Here's how you can make a difference:

Buy Handmade:

Each toy is carefully crafted with skills unique to specific regions. Choosing handmade supports artisans directly, preserving their craft and tradition.

Spread Awareness:

Share the story of these crafts with friends and family. Increased demand can create sustainable opportunities for artisans.

Collaborate with Us:

Together, we can create more avenues for these art forms to flourish. Every purchase and every effort counts in keeping the legacy of traditional Indian toys alive.
